Problem 5: Stakeholder Interests Documentation:

This section documents the interests, expectations, and influence levels of identified stakeholders for the Lost & Found Portal.

University Students: Interested in quick recovery of lost items and easy reporting process. They expect a user-friendly interface with fast matching capabilities. Influence level is High.

University Staff: Interested in simplified lost property management and reduced administrative workload. They expect reliable system operation with clear procedures. Influence level is Medium.

System Administrator: Interested in easy maintenance and system reliability. They expect secure, stable operation with minimal downtime. Influence level is Medium.

University Management: Interested in improved campus efficiency and student satisfaction. They expect cost reduction in lost property management and positive ROI. Influence level is High.

Campus Security: Interested in streamlined workflow and reduced storage of unclaimed items. They expect clear handover procedures and reliable item tracking. Influence level is Medium.

IT Department: Interested in scalable infrastructure and compliance with university IT policies. They expect smooth integration with existing campus systems. Influence level is Medium.
