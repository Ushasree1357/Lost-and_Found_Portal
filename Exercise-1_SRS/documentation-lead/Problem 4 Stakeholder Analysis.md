Problem 4: Stakeholder Analysis:
Stakeholder analysis identifies individuals and groups that are involved in or affected by the system. Understanding stakeholders helps align system requirements with real-world expectations for the Lost & Found Portal.

Stakeholder	Role	Level of Involvement	Impact on Project

University Students	Report lost/found items	High	Primary system users and determines adoption
University Staff	Occasional users and support	Medium	Ensures campus-wide effectiveness
System Administrator	System maintenance	Medium	Ensures system stability and reliability
University Management	Project oversight	High	Evaluates project success and ROI
Campus Security	Operational integration	Medium	Ensures practical workflow integration
IT Department	Technical infrastructure	Medium	Supports system deployment and maintenance
