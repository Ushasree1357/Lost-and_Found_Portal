Problem 2: Team Agreement Creation:
The Team Agreement establishes a common understanding among team members regarding roles, communication practices, decision-making, and quality expectations. This agreement ensures disciplined teamwork and professional collaboration throughout the Lost & Found Portal project lifecycle.
2.1 Team Roles and Responsibilities
• Project Manager (PM): Oversees project planning, scheduling, coordination, and ensures timely completion of all deliverables for the Lost & Found Portal.
• Documentation Lead: Prepares, maintains, and reviews all project documentation including stakeholder analysis, requirements, glossary, and ensures consistency across documents for the Lost & Found Portal.
• Lead Developer: Responsible for system design, modeling, and technical architecture of the Lost & Found Portal system.
• QA Lead: Ensures correctness of requirements, validates workflows, and prepares design validation documents for the Lost & Found Portal.
2.2 Communication Methods and Frequency
The team communicates through WhatsApp for daily coordination and Google Meet for weekly review meetings for the Lost & Found Portal project. Urgent issues are communicated immediately to ensure no delays in progress.
2.3 Decision-Making Process
Decisions are made collaboratively after discussion among team members for the Lost & Found Portal project. The Project Manager provides final approval for major technical and documentation-related decisions.
2.4 Conflict Resolution Strategy
Any conflicts arising during the Lost & Found Portal project are resolved through open discussion. If issues persist, they are escalated to the Project Manager for final resolution.
2.5 Commitment to Deadlines and Quality
All team members commit to completing assigned tasks within deadlines for the Lost & Found Portal project. Quality checks are performed before submission to ensure originality, clarity, and correctness.
