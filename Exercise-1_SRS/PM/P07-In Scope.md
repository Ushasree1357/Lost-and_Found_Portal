In-Scope Features:

•	Uploading lost/found item images in supported formats (JPG, PNG)
•	Reporting lost items with details (description, category, location, date)
•	Reporting found items with details (description, category, location found, date)
•	Searching and filtering lost/found items by keywords, category, location, and date
•	Matching lost items with found items based on description and location similarity
•	Submitting and managing claims for found items
•	Providing a user-friendly interface for students and staff
