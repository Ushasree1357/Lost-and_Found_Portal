2. Overall Description:

2.1 Product Perspective
The system is a standalone web-based application designed to support campus lost and found operations through centralized online reporting and matching.
2.2 Product Functions
•	Lost item reporting and management
•	Found item reporting and management
•	Item search and matching
•	Claim submission and verification
•	User authentication and administration
2.3 User Classes and Characteristics
•	Students: Primary users, limited technical knowledge
•	University Staff: Occasional users, varying technical knowledge
•	Administrators: Manage system operations and verify claims
2.4 Operating Environment
•	Web browser-based interface (desktop and mobile responsive)
•	Server-side processing with database storage
•	Email notification system
2.5 Constraints and Assumptions
•	Internet connectivity required for system access
•	Users must provide valid university email for registration
•	Item images must be in supported formats (JPG, PNG)
