Project Title 
Lost & Found Portal 
Project Overview:
This project aims to design and document a centralized online platform that enables university students and staff to report lost and found items, match them effectively, and streamline the recovery process to improve campus operations and user convenience.
________________________________________
Project Objectives
1.	To create an online system for reporting lost and found items
2.	To facilitate quick search and matching between lost and found reports
3.	To enable efficient claim submission and ownership verification
4.	To reduce administrative burden on campus staff and security
5.	To improve lost item recovery rates on campus
________________________________________
Scope Summary
In Scope
•	User registration and authentication
•	Lost and found item reporting with image upload
•	Search and filtering functionality
•	Claim submission and management
•	Administrator dashboard and system management
Out of Scope
•	Mobile application development
•	Payment or reward systems
•	Integration with external security systems
•	Physical item storage or delivery
